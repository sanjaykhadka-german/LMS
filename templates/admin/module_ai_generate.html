How it works

Gemini reads your document and produces a module JSON with:

  • An opening hook (story), scenario, and takeaway section
  • 2–4 content sections with bullets and role breakdowns
  • A 10-question quiz (mixed multiple-choice and true/false)

Generation takes roughly 30–40 seconds. After it finishes you'll land on the import screen with the JSON pre-filled. Review it, edit anything that looks off, then click Import.

Scanned PDFs work too — Gemini reads them with its vision model. The upload cap is 20 MB per file.
